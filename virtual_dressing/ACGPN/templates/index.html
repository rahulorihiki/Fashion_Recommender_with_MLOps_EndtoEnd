Virtual Try On

Your Personalized Fashion Haven.

Explore, create, and indulge in a world of fashion possibilities with our interactive virtual wardrobe.

Modules in our Model


1) Semantic Generation Module

This module generates detailed semantic descriptions of clothing items, including style, color, material, and fit, enabling accurate virtual representation and personalized styling recommendations.

2) Clothes Wraping Module

The clothes wrapping module applies advanced algorithms to seamlessly drape virtual garments onto user-provided images, enabling realistic virtual try-ons and accurate visualization of clothing items.

3) Content Fusion Module

The content fusion module intelligently combines user preferences, current fashion trends, and personalized styling suggestions to create cohesive and inspiring fashion content, delivering a curated and engaging virtual dressing experience.

Guidelines

Step 1

Go to Try Out Room section. Upload your image by clicking on "Choose File" button and select your image. Your posture should be erect with a plane background.


Note: The minimum size of the image must be 8KB and maximum limit is 2MB.

Step 2

Upload your desired cloth image by clicking on "Choose File" button and select the image of the cloth you wish to try it on yourself.



Note: The minimum size of the image must be 8KB and maximum limit is 2MB.

Step 3

Click on the "Submit" button to see the result.
